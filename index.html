About Me

My journey in life has certainly been quite a ride. Shortly after receiving bachelor's degree in Criminal Justice from Penn State University, I moved to New Orleans where I worked as a legal assistant for 2 law firms. Living in a city with such a vibrant music scene, I transformed from an amateur professional to a working professional. During this time, I continued to work as a legal assistant during the day, while performing for audiences at night. Eventually I discovered that I had a gift for teaching music and that would define my life for the next 20 years. I received a Master's Degree in music performance from the University of New Orleans in 2005. That event, along with Hurricane Katrina, prompted me to find a new home. I moved to Seattle shortly thereafter and began teaching privately as well as at Edmonds Community College. Being surrounded by the tech industry and having a partner who is a developer, I couldn't help but want to learn coding!
